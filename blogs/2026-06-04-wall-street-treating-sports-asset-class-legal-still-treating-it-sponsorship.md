Major sporting events like the 2026 FIFA World Cup and LA28 are creating significant compliance challenges for financial institutions and corporations as Wall Street treats sports as an investment asset class. Legal and operational teams must address multilingual consumer communications, cross-border privacy regulations, and investor disclosure requirements that vary by jurisdiction, issues often overlooked in standard sponsorship deals.
