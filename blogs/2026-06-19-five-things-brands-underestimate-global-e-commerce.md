Brands expanding internationally often overlook five operational realities: localization for conversion (including local payment methods like Sofort, Giropay, and iDEAL), early compliance integration (France's extended producer responsibility, EU textile labeling), escalating post-launch content volume, fragmented internal workflows across platforms like Zalando, Tmall, and Mercado Libre, and sustained post-launch operations. The article argues success requires scalable localization processes rather than treating launch as the endpoint.
